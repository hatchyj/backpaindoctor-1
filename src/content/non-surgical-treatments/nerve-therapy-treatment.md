When we suffer from past injuries, our bodies may not heal properly, causing nerves to become pinched or restricted by scar tissue, resulting in pain and numbness in our joints and tissues. Some patients experience sharp or hot bursts of pain rushing through an area of the body. Fortunately, these pains and conditions can be treated with nerve treatments.

Our regenerative experts use a simple and gentle injection series to bathe the affected nerve completely. This solution effectively turns off negative pain receptors and reprograms the nerve's normal function.
